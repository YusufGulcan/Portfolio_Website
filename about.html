I graduated from one of the leading universities -Bogazici University- in Turkey. I studied Economics and International Trade. Since my graduation, I have been self-learning data science disciplines. I attended a couple of data science camps which were organized by Bogazici University.

Before Data Science

As I was still studying, I started working as a Search Engine Evaluator in a company called TELUS International. My main responsibility there was to evaluate how well the Google search results satisfied the user needs. Also I evaluated the customer targeting performance of Google Advertisement algorithms. After working there about 14 months, I needed focus on my graduation and left the company.

After Graduation

After completing my study, I started working as a data curator in an IT company, Veeva Systems. My responsibility was to collect data available on web about the entities, individuals and events related to American Health Sector. I intensely used Office Excel in this period. At this point, I started my data science path. I learned SQL, Python and R programming languages. After developing a technical base, I began sharpening my skills with projects in different areas. Some of the topics of my projects were:

  • Customer Segmentation
  • Churn Prediction
  • Stock/Cryptocurrency Price Prediction
  • Computer Vision
  • Data Collection
  • Recommendation Engine
  • Product Price Prediction
I mainly used the following Python libraries in my projects;
  • Pandas
  • Numpy
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
  • Scikit Learn
  • Tensorflow
  • Pytorch
  • Selenium
  • BeautifulSoup
